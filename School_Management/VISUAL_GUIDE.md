## ğŸ¨ Visual Hierarchy

### Priority Levels
```
Level 1 (Highest):
  - Page titles (Large, bold, colored)
  - Primary action buttons (Prominent, colored)
  - Important stats (Large numbers, gradients)

Level 2 (Medium):
  - Section headings (Medium, semibold)
  - Secondary buttons (Standard size)
  - Key information (Regular weight)

Level 3 (Lower):
  - Labels (Small, uppercase)
  - Tertiary actions (Links, small buttons)
  - Supplementary info (Gray text)
```
